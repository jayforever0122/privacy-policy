Frequently Asked Questions

Does Wall & Ceiling Takeoff work offline?
Yes. All your projects, wall/ceiling takeoffs, and the built-in supplier product library are stored locally on your device and work fully offline.

Which suppliers' systems are included?
Studco, Rondo, Siniat, CSR (Red Book), and Armstrong wall and ceiling systems, sourced from their published technical data.

How do I change the app's language?
Go to Settings inside the app and pick your language from the Language section. The app also follows your device's system language automatically the first time you open it.

How do subscriptions work?
Subscriptions are billed and managed entirely through Apple. You can view, change, or cancel your subscription any time in your device's Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.
