Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Wall & Ceiling Takeoff ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use the App on iOS or macOS.

Summary

Wall & Ceiling Takeoff is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not operate any servers that collect, store, or process your project data, and we do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Specifically:

  • Project data (projects, wall types, ceiling types, takeoff items, material calculations, and settings) is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to us or any third party.
  • We do not require an account, email address, or any sign-in to use the App.
  • We do not use tracking, advertising identifiers, or third-party analytics services.

Subscriptions & Payments

Wall & Ceiling Takeoff offers optional auto-renewing subscriptions (Pro and Max tiers) through Apple's In-App Purchase / StoreKit system. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple:

  • We never see or store your payment card details.
  • Apple may collect information necessary to process your purchase in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Subscription status is verified locally on-device via StoreKit; no purchase or billing data is sent to us.

Data Storage & Backup

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud or device backups (a setting controlled by your device's operating system, not by this App), your project data may be included in that backup, subject to Apple's own iCloud/backup privacy terms.

Children's Privacy

Wall & Ceiling Takeoff is a professional construction estimating tool not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date above.
